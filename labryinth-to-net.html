The Project

Client wanted new website designed for her Marriage and Family Therapy practice. My task was to create the branding, design the website, code the website, and design business cards for her company.

"Like an Orchid"

After creating an initial template of the website and using the copy provided by the client as a starting-off point, she and I discussed the aesthesis of the site. She stated that she wanted the color palette to be “like an orchid,” so after some research together she picked out a photo and we based the color palette of the website from the image.

From there I used the imagery and color palette to create her business cards and am in process of creating printed brochures.
