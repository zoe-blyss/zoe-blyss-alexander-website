About Zoe

Zoe Blyss Alexander is a web designer currently living in Minneapolis, Minnesota. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurial Studies at the Minneapolis College of Art and Design (yes, she did go to art school to get a Bachelor of Science in business). Zoe particularly enjoys combining front-end development tools with art to push the current definitions of what websites and art can be. In her spare time Zoe likes read, play point-and-click interactive games (while analyzing how she would create them herself), and sip lattes at her favorite café.

The French Kiss Life Homepage Case-Study

Final project for my Web Design class Fall 2019. Assignment was to replicate the home-page of a website. First tasked with replicating the component of site on a separate page using the BEM method, then compose all of atoms and molecules together to create the main page. Original homepage of site I replicated, “French Kiss Life,” included for comparison.
